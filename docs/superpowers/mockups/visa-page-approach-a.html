The Mission
Chain Bridge, first night

Budapest received us like a city that had been expecting us — Haris in his flat cap and aviators, conspiratorial at every turn, and Vedad grinning beside bronze statues and barbershop signs as though the city had been arranged specifically for his amusement.

The thermal baths became a recurring motif — steam rising from turquoise pools while they debated whether the city's cafe culture rivalled Vienna's. Spoiler: it did, at half the price and twice the charm. The ruin bars sealed the verdict before the second night was out.
