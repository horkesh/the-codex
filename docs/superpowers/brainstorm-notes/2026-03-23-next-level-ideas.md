# Next Level Feature Ideas

**Date**: 2026-03-23
**Status**: Brainstorm — awaiting prioritisation

---

## 1. Live Mode
When all gents are at the same location (GPS proximity), the app enters a special shared mode. Real-time shared photo feed, live entry creation together, everyone sees what everyone posts instantly. Like a shared camera roll for the night. Next morning, AI auto-generates the entry from the collective photos.

## 2. The Bet Board
Make bets with each other. "I bet Bass can't eat a full T-bone in under 10 minutes." Track wagers, outcomes, win/loss records. AI referee for disputed outcomes. Integrated into entries — when you log a steak night, active bets auto-resolve.

## 3. The Map Game
Scratch-off world map. Countries/cities you've visited together get "scratched" (revealed in gold). Visual progress toward goals like "Visit 20 countries together" or "Hit every Balkan capital."

## 4. Gent Radio
A continuous audio stream that shuffles through all noir narrations + mission soundtracks. Put it on in the background while cooking or driving. Your own private podcast of memories. Crossfade between tracks, show current entry context.
