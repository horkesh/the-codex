# Vision — The Codex

**The Codex** is the product/app name. **The Gents Chronicles** is the external brand name.

## What it is

A private lifestyle companion app for three friends. A living record of everything they do — trips, nights out, steaks, PlayStation sessions, curated gatherings — documented in a way that looks extraordinary and can be shared instantly in Instagram-ready format.

Three users. One chronicle. Infinite style.

## The five pillars

### I. The Chronicle
Every experience logged. Every entry has a type: Mission (trip), Night Out, The Table (steak), The Pitch (PlayStation), The Toast (cocktail party), Gathering (curated event), or Interlude (anything else). Each type has its own visual card language. Claude writes the narrative — "The Lore" — a cinematic paragraph for every entry.

Running lifetime stats are visible at all times: missions, countries, nights out, steaks, PS5 wins, gatherings hosted, people met.

### II. The Passport
The Gents' passport concept, fully alive in digital form. Every Mission stamps the passport with a city/country seal. Achievements unlock special stamps. Diplomatic stamps mark extraordinary moments. The Passport is the showpiece — the thing you pull out to wow someone you just met. A beautiful, full-screen experience.

### III. The Studio
Export anything in Instagram-ready format. Mission carousels, night out cards, steak verdicts, PS5 match results, passport pages, gathering invite cards, annual Wrapped. Everything uses the brand design system. You log, then export. No design tools needed.

### IV. The Circle
People the Gents meet. Logged to entries, browsable as a network. Names, Instagram handles, where and when they were met. Custom labels per person (e.g., "legend", "BACHATA", "recurring"). Private per-Gent notes. The Budapest list is the seed data.

### V. The Gathering
Curated events — villa weekends, cocktail parties, intimate gatherings — managed end-to-end inside The Codex. Pre-event: build the guest list, generate an invite card, share via WhatsApp or a public RSVP link. At the party: a live QR guest book — guests scan, pick a cocktail, leave a message. Post-event: add photos, generate Lore, publish as a full Chronicle entry. The Gathering transforms from invitation to legacy.

---

## Design philosophy

Mobile-first. The "wow" moments — Passport reveal, invite card animation, QR guest book — happen on mobile. Desktop works well but is not the focus.

Not a scrapbook. A monument. Dark, luxurious, gold-accented. The kind of app you open and feel proud of. The kind of thing you show to someone new and watch their face change.

High class. No compromise.

## Users

- Three fixed users: the three Gents (each with their own login and persona)
- Data is shared — one chronicle, three contributors
- All Chronicle entries are visible to all three Gents — no selective sharing
- Each Gent has private notes on Circle people that only they can see
- Each Gent has an alias: Keys & Cocktails, Beard & Bass, Lorekeeper

## What it is not

- A social media platform (private, for the three of them)
- A travel booking or planning tool
- A public-facing app for strangers (though public pages exist for Gathering RSVP and QR guest book — these require no login)
- Another version of The Toast (that's for parties; this is for legacy)
